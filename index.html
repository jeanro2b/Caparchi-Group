THE BEST LASER AND SUB THERMIC MANUFACTURING

For over 30 years, Caparchi Group has specialized in the design and production of turnkey laser and sub thermic micromachining, medical and aesthetic solutions. Our products range from flexible R&D workstations to robust 24/7 Industrial configurations that cover all types of applications.

Caparchi Group is known as Sub thermic and Laser experts with unequaled experience in deep-UV excimer laser processing, pulse light technology and more recently, picosecond and femtosecond laser processing. The Company is distinguished by its core competencies including in-house optical design capability, and Caparchi Group’s proprietary laser processing and sub thermic processing technologies. Our knowledge-base and resources make it possible to push system performance beyond industry quality and efficiency standards. Since 2010 Caparchi Group has also developed high engineering skills in electromagnetism solutions applied to human muscles.
